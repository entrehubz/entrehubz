About Us

Who Should Join

We welcome all individuals passionate about starting their entrepreneurial journey. However, there is a key requirement: You must be genuinely interested in becoming an entrepreneur. Entrepreneurship requires hard work, dedication, and perseverance. If you are not ready to fully commit to this journey, we respectfully ask that you not join our program. Our goal is to support only those who are committed to putting in the effort for their own success.

Let’s Make Your Dream Business a Reality!
Free Webinars and Awareness Programs

ENTREHUBZ Solutions is committed to making entrepreneurship accessible to everyone. For those who cannot join our paid programs due to financial constraints, we offer free webinars designed to provide insights into entrepreneurship, grant schemes, funding opportunities, and more. These sessions also promote the entrepreneurial ecosystem in India and Kerala, helping you take your first steps toward building your dream business.

Collaboration with Kerala Startup Mission

We connect aspiring entrepreneurs with the Kerala Startup Mission (KSUM), providing awareness about various grant schemes, funding opportunities, and industrial generation grants available from both the state and central government. This includes programs like NIDHI PRAYAS and other schemes that can help you access the funding you need to launch your business.

Support Across All Industries

ENTREHUBZ Solutions supports a variety of industries, including the service industry, production industry, and more. We provide strong support during the early stages of startup development, ensuring you’re on the right path to securing seed funding. Once you’ve received your seed funding, our support continues with valuable advice and insights, but the main focus is on helping you get to that critical seed fund stage.

More Than Certificates

At ENTREHUBZ Solutions, we don’t believe in handing out certificates. While many programs offer certificates at the end of their courses, we focus on something far more meaningful: your business in your hands. We empower you to build your micro, small, or medium enterprise (MSME) from the ground up. Our goal is to help you become a successful entrepreneur, not just give you a certificate.
